Circuito

Dat del problema

  • $$\text{Resistenze pari} ~ 10K \Omega$$
  • $$\text{Resistenze dispari} ~ 3K \Omega$$
  • $$V_1 ~ 10 ~ V$$

Analisi circuitale

Troviamo la resistenza equivalente

  • $$ R_{eq} = [(R_6 \parallel R_7 ~+~ R_8) ~+~ (R_4 \parallel R_5) ~\parallel~ (R_2 \parallel R_3)] ~+~ R_1$$
  • $$ R_{eq} = [(10000 \parallel 3000 ~+~ 10000) ~+~ (10000 \parallel 30000) ~\parallel~ (10000 \parallel 3000)] ~+~ 3000$$
  • $$ R_{eq} = [(\frac{10000 ~ \cdot ~ 13000}{10000 ~+~ 13000}) ~+~ (\frac{10000 ~ \cdot ~ 3000}{10000 ~+~ 3000}) ~\parallel~ (\frac{10000 ~ \cdot ~ 3000}{10000 ~+~ 3000})] ~+~ 3000$$
  • $$ R_{eq} = [5652 ~+~ (2307) ~\parallel~ (2307)] ~+~ 3000$$
  • $$ R_{eq} = [5652 ~+~ \frac{2307 ~\cdot~ 2307}{2307 ~+~ 2307}] ~+~ 3000$$
  • $$ R_{eq} = [5652 ~+~ 1153] ~+~ 3000$$
  • $$ R_{eq} = 6805 ~+~ 3000$$
  • $$ R_{eq} = 9805 ~ \Omega$$

Troviamo la corrente 1

  • $$I = \frac{V}{R}$$
  • $$ I_1 = \frac{V_1}{R_{eq}}$$
  • $$ I_1 = \frac{10}{9805}$$
  • $$ I_1 = 1 ~ mA$$

Calcliamo la tensione nel tratto AB

  • $$V_{AB} = I_1 ~\cdot~ R_1 ~+~ I_2 ~\cdot~ R_2 = 0$$
  • $$1 ~\cdot~ 3000 ~+~ I_2 ~\cdot~ 10000 = 0$$
  • $$1 ~\cdot~ 3000 ~+~ 10000 = I_2$$
  • $$1 ~\cdot~ 3000 ~+~ 10000 = 13000$$
